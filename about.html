hello, komusta!

My name is Kim, the human behind KP Lettering.

I’m a Filipinx-Canadian artist based in Toronto, Canada, who’s passionate about design, travel, and hot Cheetos.

I picked up my first pen in 2017, and I have been practicing ever since. My work is heavily inspired by organic elements and nature, since travelling and exploring is what inspires me the most.

The outcome of my love for lettering has enabled me to launch modern calligraphy services for events. So whether it be for your wedding, birthday, or shower, I’d love to make your special day more memorable with a touch of hand lettering. For commission inquiries you can contact me via email.

Stay curious & eat Cheetos, friends!

~ Kim
